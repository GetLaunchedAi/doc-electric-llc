Professional New Construction Wiring


Complete Electrical Installation for Your New Build


DOC Electric LLC brings over 20 years of experience in new construction wiring. From rough-in to final connections, we provide comprehensive electrical services for residential and commercial projects. Our team ensures code compliance, quality workmanship, and clear communication throughout every phase of construction.



Our New Construction Process

  1. Site consultation: review plans, determine electrical needs, and create specifications
  2. Rough-in installation: install conduit, wiring, boxes, and panels before drywall
  3. Final connections: install fixtures, outlets, switches, and complete panel connections

New Construction Services

  • Complete electrical system design and installation
  • Service panel installation and upgrades
  • Outlet and switch installation throughout the building
  • Lighting fixture installation
  • Dedicated circuits for appliances and equipment
  • Low-voltage wiring for security, data, and communication systems
