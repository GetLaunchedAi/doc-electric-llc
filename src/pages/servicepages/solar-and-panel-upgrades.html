Professional Solar Electrical & Panel Upgrades


PV Wiring, Inverters, Utility Tie-In + Modernized Panels


DOC Electric LLC handles solar electrical scopes—including inverter placement, AC/DC wiring, rapid shutdown, grounding, labeling, and interconnection—as well as panel upgrades to add capacity and improve safety. We coordinate inspections and utility approvals for a smooth process.



Our Solar Electrical Process

  1. Review plans: array layout, inverter specs, and interconnection method
  2. Install: run conduit/wire, set disconnects, mount inverter, label
  3. Commission: test, configure monitoring, and meet inspector requirements
